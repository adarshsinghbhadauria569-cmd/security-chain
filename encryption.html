🛡 Advanced Encryption

Encryption Basics

Encryption converts readable information into coded data using mathematical algorithms. Only authorized users with the correct key can decrypt and access the original information.

Modern Encryption Algorithms

Cybersecurity systems use algorithms like AES-256, RSA, and SHA-256. These algorithms protect banking systems, military communication, and blockchain networks.

Importance of Encryption

Encryption protects sensitive data from hackers during storage and internet transmission. It is essential for secure websites, online payments, and digital identity systems.
